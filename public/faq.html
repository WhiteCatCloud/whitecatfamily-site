Privacy & Data

No. WhiteCat sees that a device visited "youtube.com" — it never sees what video was watched, what was searched, or what was typed. We collect domain-level counts (blocked or allowed) to show you usage insights. We never read the content of your family's internet traffic, and we never sell data to advertisers.

Your browsing data is never routed through or copied to our servers. The router makes blocking decisions locally — only metadata (device status, domain block counts, firmware version) is synced to our secure US-based servers to power the app and push updates. We never see the content of what your family visits online.
