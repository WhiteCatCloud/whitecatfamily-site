🎯 Instagram — The Illusion of Perfection

Instagram isn't just pictures — it's a perfectly engineered dopamine trap.

👉 Infinite scroll means you never "finish."
👉 Likes and hearts give just enough validation to keep you posting.
👉 Filters and face-tuning create impossible beauty standards.

Meta's own leaked research (Frances Haugen, 2021) showed:
🔹 32% of teen girls said Instagram worsened their body image.
🔹 Many reported higher anxiety, depression, and eating disorders.

Yet the platform doubled down, because more screen time = more ad money.

It's not just kids. Adults are caught in the same loop of comparing their real lives to curated perfection. The result? Addiction, insecurity, and hours stolen every day.

Here's the thing: the problem isn't "kids being weak" — it's systems built to exploit psychology. And systems can be fixed.
