From One Parent to Another

"I built White Cat because I was losing the battle."

As a security engineer with 20 years of experience, I thought setting up parental controls would be easy. But I soon realized my own children were finding loopholes—VPNs, encrypted DNS, and proxy sites—faster than I could block them.

Most routers are "black boxes" that prioritize data collection over actual security. I wanted something different: a router that was open-source, completely private, and actually bypass-proof.

White Cat is the result of that mission. It’s built on hardened, transparent firmware that puts the power back where it belongs—in your hands, not the cloud.
