How It Works?

WhiteCat is a powerful Wi-Fi 6 router with open-source firmware and built-in parental controls. It blocks bad stuff before it reaches your kids, keeps your devices safe, and quietly enforces the rules you set — so your home internet just behaves.

01

Powerful hardware

Blazing-fast Wi-Fi 6, a 64-bit dual-core processor, and plenty of memory let you stream, game, and work without glitches or slowdowns. Everything — filtering, blocking, device detection — runs in real time. Silent, fanless design that disappears into your home but never compromises performance.

02

Open-source firmware you can trust.

WhiteCat runs on hardened, open-source firmware instead of a closed black box. Security researchers and power users can inspect it — no hidden backdoors, no mystery code, no data sent to random servers in other countries. You get enterprise-grade foundations, tuned for real families.

03

An app built for non-techy parents.

No IP addresses, No firewall jargon. You see people and services: “Emma’s iPad”, “TikTok”, “YouTube”, “Xbox” , "Smart TV". Tap to block, tap to pause, drag to adjust bedtimes or homework time. That’s it.

04

WhiteCat ecosystem: router, app and cloud

The router enforces the rules in your home. The cloud keeps firmware, malware filters, and service lists up to date — and lets you control everything remotely. You stay in charge; WhiteCat handles the background work without you lifting a finger.
